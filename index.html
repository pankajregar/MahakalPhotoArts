About Mahakal Photo Arts

Founded in 2015, Mahakal Photo Arts has grown from a passion project into one of Rajasthan's most sought-after wedding photography studios. Our journey began with a simple belief: every wedding tells a unique love story that deserves to be captured beautifully.

With over 500 weddings captured across India and internationally, we've perfected the art of blending into your celebration while capturing every precious moment—from the grand ceremonies to the intimate glances that make your day truly special.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking with us

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October-March). However, we understand that sometimes plans come together quickly, so reach out regardless of your timeline and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Photo sneak peeks are delivered within 48-72 hours. Full edited photo galleries are delivered within 3-4 weeks. Wedding films take 6-8 weeks for completion. Premium packages include priority delivery with faster turnaround times.

We use professional-grade Canon and Sony cameras with premium lenses, DJI drones for aerial shots, gimbals for smooth video footage, and professional lighting equipment. All our gear is backed up to ensure no technical issues affect your special day.
